Alumni

The Liturgical Choir and its predecessor the Chapel Choir have served the Notre Dame community since 1973. Countless men and women have taken part in the Choir's ministry of enhancing prayer through song, using their voices to praise the Glory of God. As a choir, we sing together, bringing many voices into one full, melodic prayer; and while praying together, we have formed a family that spans over thirty-five years. The University maintains contact information for all Liturgical Choir alumni. To connect with fellow Liturgical Choir alumni, follow the following steps.

  1. Log onto Irish Online.
  2. Perform a search by clicking on "Directory Advanced Search" in the left-hand navigation bar. You can search for alumni by Contact Information, Employment Information, Family Information, Notre Dame Information, or Other Information.
  3. In order to search for all Liturgical Choir alumni, click on "Notre Dame Information" and then select "Music-Liturgical Choir member" from the drop-down menu next to "Student Activity."
  4. To narrow the search, add in other search terms such as first/last name or class year.
  5. Ensure that your Irish Online profile is up to date so that other alumni will be able to contact you!

If you have any questions or concerns regarding alumni matters, send an e-mail to mchambe3@nd.edu.
